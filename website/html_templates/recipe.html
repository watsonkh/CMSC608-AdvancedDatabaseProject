This is a brief description of the recipe, including its origin, flavor profile, or any interesting facts.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the flour and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add the eggs, vanilla extract, and milk. Stir until smooth.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Let it cool before serving. Enjoy!
